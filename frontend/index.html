What Maatri does

Everything she needs,
in one gentle place

🩺
AI Risk Scoring

Enter Hb, BP, and blood sugar. Maatri applies WHO/MOHFW thresholds and instantly flags Green, Amber, or Red — with a plain-language reason in your language.

🎙️
Language Input

ANMs speak symptoms aloud in any regional language. Whisper-powered STT transcribes and maps to structured clinical fields in real time.

📷
Lab Report OCR

Photograph any printed lab report. PaddleOCR auto-extracts Hb, sugar, and BP values directly into the patient record — no manual typing needed.

📍
Village Risk Heatmap

See high-risk clusters across your PHC area at a glance. Pulsing red circles mark villages needing urgent attention — updated in real time.

📲
WhatsApp Referral

One tap sends a structured clinical summary to the nearest doctor — in proper SOAP format, with vitals, risk level, and suggested action — translated to local language.

🌙
Offline First

No internet? No problem. Maatri stores all records locally using SQLite and syncs to the PHC server the moment connectivity returns.
